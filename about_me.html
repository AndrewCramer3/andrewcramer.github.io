About Me

Who Am I?

           Andrew Cramer is a highly creative and passion-driven developer dedicated to crafting memorable video game experiences. As a programmer, he specializes in AI and possesses a deep love for story-telling and narrative design. Andrew has actively participated in various Game Jams, Semester Projects, and is currently immersed in the development of Gnomad, his senior project.

           Beyond his coding endeavors, Andrew is an avid music enthusiast, having enjoyed a rich musical journey over an extended period. His top three music artists right now are Tyler, The Creator, Billy Joel, and The Cure. He is also pursuing a hobby in music-making; learning how to create and compose musical pieces that can impact others in some way.

           In addition to his commitment to the gaming world and music, Andrew indulges in a variety of hobbies such as cooking, playing pickleball or some sport and immersing himself in movies. His curiosity knows no bounds as he actively seeks new interests and endeavors to engage in, ensuring that his passion for exploration and creativity remains a driving force in both his personal and professional life.
