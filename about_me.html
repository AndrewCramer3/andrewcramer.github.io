About Me

Who Am I?

           Andrew Cramer is a highly creative and passion-driven developer dedicated to crafting memorable video game experiences. As a programmer, he specializes in game design and possesses a deep love for story-telling and narrative design. Andrew has actively participated in various Game Jams, Semester Projects, and is currently immersed in the development of Gnomad, his senior project.

           Beyond his coding endeavors, Andrew has a talent for creative thinking. Wanting to explore his talents and learn how to create new ideas to share with the world, he took to learning music and writing. His father helped emerse him in music from a young age, teaching him guitar and he also tried picking up piano just recently. Both of these things helped kickstart a want to start making music which you can see throughout his portfolio. And since being out of college full-time, he decided to finally take a stab at writing short stories and figure out how to express ideas that will linger in peoples minds a little longer than they would have otherwise thought.

           In addition to his commitment to the gaming world and creative expression, Andrew indulges in a variety of hobbies such as cooking, rock climbing and immersing himself in movies. His curiosity knows no bounds as he actively seeks new interests and endeavors to engage in, ensuring that his passion for exploration and creativity remains a driving force in both his personal and professional life.
